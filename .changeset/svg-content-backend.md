svg-content backend brings live-DOM refraction to Safari and Firefox: a counter-positioned clone of the designated backdrop element is refracted with filter url() displacement inside the glass, kept in sync via MutationObserver and dom-sync. New `backdrop` option (Element or selector) on attach and the `<liquid-glass backdrop>` attribute.
