webgl-scene backend: full glass optics in a WebGL2 shader (SDF shape, bevel refraction, chromatic dispersion, poisson blur, saturation, tint, frost noise, specular rim) rendered over a scene image aligned with a reference element. New `sceneImage` option, explicit-only backend selection via autoSelect, and `backend`/`scene-image` attributes on the element.
